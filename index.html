About Me


My name is Roman Penn, thrill seeker, optimist, and an all around joyful person. I grew up in Shreveport, LA with two amazing parents and an ok sister(just kidding she's pretty awesome). My love for computers started at a very young age but was never fully pursued until much later in life. However, I feel like it's never too late to follow up on your passions you have to put on hold from time to time.

My career started where everyone's started in highschool. My first job was working as a cashier at a local water park called Watertown USA(it has since shutdown and is now owned by Splash Mountain). The very next summer I would become a manager of food services at the ripe old age of 16. I recieved my next job at Krispy Kreme Doughnuts(which comically I am now working for again just at a different location) at the age 17. I graduated from Captain Shreve Highschool in 2010 at the age of 17 and decided not to wait until I was 18 to join the Marine Corps(cause when you want something why wait?!?!?). So I ended up leaving for basic less than a month after my graduation. After all of my initial training was completed (bootcamp,MCT, MOS School) I finally arrived at MCAS Miramar at the begininng of 2011. By the ending of 2012 I had been accepted as a Marine Embassy Security Guard(MSG for short) and I got to my first post in Capetown, South Africa. My second post took me to Paris, France and the third to Erbil, Iraq.

I got out of the Corps back in 2016 and I've enjoyed my time taking on many different jobs! I've been able to become a cop, waiter, uber driver, truck driver, real estate agent, and even a car salesman. So I've gotten a little taste of everything. However, I feel that now is finally the time to pursue a career that I've dreamed about since childhood in the wonderful world of computers. To be completely honest I had no idea where to start. I just knew that I wanted a job around computers and people who love them as much as I do.
